About Me

Jing Lyu focuses on UX Design and makes New Media Art.

I am a designer currently focusing on UX Design and Research at HATLab and Tangible Visualization Group, where I learned the need to design and execute studies that address both user behavior and attitudes, using the right methodology for the right questions through proper research.

I studied Communications Design at Pratt Institute, New York. This is where I am adept at communicating with users, figuring out user needs from their heart, analyzing design tasks and solutions while identifying real brand personality.
